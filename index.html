Spelling Error
My dad took lots of photos and my mom buyed souvenirs.
My dad took lots of photos while my mom bought souvenirs.
💡 "Buyed" is incorrect — "buy" is an irregular verb; past tense is "bought". Added "while" to create a compound sentence. 不规则动词buy的过去式为bought，非buyed。
Weak Expression
The roaring sound of the water was incredible and I couldn't believe how big it was.
The thunderous roar of the falls was breathtaking; I had never imagined water on such a colossal scale.
💡 Replace vague "incredible / big" with precise adjectives. Use a semicolon to join two related independent clauses. 用精确词汇替换模糊形容词，分号连接两个独立从句。
Vague Ending
It was very beautiful and I enjoyed it a lot.
The entire experience was awe-inspiring, and I left with a deep appreciation for nature's power.
💡 "Very beautiful" and "enjoyed it a lot" are overused closings. End with a specific reflection or insight to leave a stronger impression. 结尾应有具体感悟，避免空洞的"very beautiful"。
Missing Transition
We ate dinner at a restaurant near the falls.
As evening descended, we settled into a cozy restaurant overlooking the illuminated falls.
💡 Add a time-setting opener ("As evening descended") to signal the shift from daytime sightseeing to dinner. Creates scene continuity. 用时间状语从句引导场景过渡，增加叙事连贯性。
Vocabulary Upgrade
Last summer, my family went to Niagara Falls.
Last summer, my family embarked on a memorable trip to Niagara Falls.
💡 "Went to" is too plain for an opening sentence. "Embarked on" signals the essay's importance and hooks the reader from the first line. 开篇句用"embarked on"替代"went to"，立即提升文章档次。
Run-on / Coordination
My dad took lots of photos and my mom buyed souvenirs and we had a great time.
While my father carefully documented every moment through his camera, my mother selected souvenirs — and together, we created lasting memories.
💡 Three clauses joined by "and and" creates a run-on. Use a "while" clause + dash structure for sophistication. 三个"and"连接的句子是流水句，用while从句和破折号结构改善。
V1 — Original
Last summer, my family went to Niagara Falls. The roaring sound of the water was incredible and I couldn't believe how big it was. My dad took lots of photos and my mom buyed souvenirs. We ate dinner at a restaurant near the falls. It was very beautiful and I enjoyed it a lot.
V2 — Refined
Last summer, my family visited Niagara Falls. The thunderous roar of the water was breathtaking; I had never imagined a waterfall of such colossal scale. My dad took lots of photos while my mom bought souvenirs. We ate dinner at a restaurant near the falls. It was a truly awe-inspiring experience and I left with lasting memories.
V3 — Elevated (+1 Level)
Last summer, my family embarked on a memorable journey to Niagara Falls — a spectacle that surpassed every expectation. Standing at the railing, enveloped in cool mist, I felt an almost overwhelming sense of nature's raw power; I had never imagined water on such a colossal scale. While my father meticulously documented each moment through his camera lens, my mother selected keepsakes with quiet deliberateness. As evening descended, we settled into a cozy restaurant overlooking the illuminated falls. The entire experience left me with a profound appreciation for the natural world.
